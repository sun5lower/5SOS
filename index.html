Hi, we are 5 Seconds Of Summer! We are an Australian pop rock band from Sydney, New South Wales, formed in 2011. We started out on YouTube, covering other artist songs, but got our break when toured the world with "One direction" in 2013. Since we've released 3 studio albums and currently touring in North America!
